Glossary of Terms

Key terms and definitions used throughout this tome.

Tome:
A large, scholarly book.
Economy of Thought:
The principle of conveying ideas with clarity, precision, and conciseness, avoiding unnecessary complexity.
Minimalist Aesthetic:
A design approach that emphasizes simplicity and the removal of superfluous elements.
